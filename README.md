# AtliQ Hardware Sales and Finance Analytics

A business analytics project developed using Microsoft Excel, Power Query, and Power Pivot to analyze sales performance, financial metrics, customer behavior, product trends, and market profitability.

## Overview

This project demonstrates how Excel can be used as a business intelligence platform to transform raw data into actionable insights. The analysis covers multiple business dimensions, including customers, products, markets, and financial performance across fiscal years.

## Reports Included

| Report | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Customer Net Sales Performance | Customer revenue growth analysis |
| Top 10 Products | Highest-performing products by sales |
| Top 5 and Bottom 5 Products | Product demand comparison |
| New Products - 2021 | Performance of newly launched products |
| Products Division Level | Division-wise sales contribution |
| Top 5 Countries Net Sales | Country-level revenue analysis |
| Market Performance vs Target | Actual sales versus business targets |
| P & L Fiscal Year | Annual profit and loss statement |
| P & L Fiscal Months | Monthly financial performance trends |
| P & L Market | Market-level profitability analysis |
| GM% by Quarters (Sub-Zone) | Regional gross margin analysis |

## Key Business Insights

- India generated the highest net sales among all markets.
- Personal Computers recorded the strongest growth among product divisions.
- Multiple new product launches significantly contributed to FY 2021 revenue.
- Gross margins varied considerably across regions and sub-zones.
- Several markets remained below their target sales performance, creating opportunities for strategic improvements.
