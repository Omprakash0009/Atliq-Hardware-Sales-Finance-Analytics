# AtliQ Sales & Finance Analytics

## Overview

This project delivers a complete Sales and Finance Analytics solution for AtliQ Hardware using Microsoft Excel, Power Query, and Power Pivot. The objective was to transform raw business data into actionable insights that support strategic planning, performance monitoring, and informed decision-making.

The reports cover customers, products, divisions, markets, profitability, and gross margin performance across multiple fiscal years.

---

## Business Scenario

AtliQ Hardware manufactures and distributes a diverse range of products, including personal computers, peripherals, storage devices, and accessories. The company operates through retail partners and online marketplaces to reach customers across global markets.

As the business expanded, leadership required detailed analytical reports to understand sales trends, monitor financial health, and identify growth opportunities.

---

## Project Goals

### Sales Analytics

- Analyze year-over-year product growth.
- Identify the top and bottom performing products.
- Evaluate division-wise revenue contributions.
- Measure the success of newly launched products.
- Determine the highest revenue-generating countries.

### Finance Analytics

- Track customer revenue performance.
- Compare actual sales against market targets.
- Analyze annual and monthly profit and loss statements.
- Evaluate profitability across global markets.
- Monitor gross margin performance by region and quarter.

## Sales Reports

| Report | Business Objective |
|----------|-------------------|
| Top 10 Products | Identify products with the highest growth |
| Products Division Level | Compare revenue across divisions |
| Top 5 & Bottom 5 Products | Analyze product demand |
| New Products - 2021 | Evaluate new product performance |
| Top 5 Countries Net Sales | Identify leading markets |

### Key Findings

- Personal Computers achieved the highest growth, increasing from ₹40.1M to ₹165.8M.
- Accessories & Peripherals generated ₹338.4M in FY 2021.
- AQ Mx NB recorded the strongest year-over-year growth.
- Sixteen newly introduced products contributed ₹176.2M in revenue.
- India remained the largest market with ₹161.3M in net sales.

---

## Financial Reports

| Report | Business Objective |
|----------|-------------------|
| Customer Net Sales Performance | Analyze customer revenue growth |
| Market Performance vs Target | Measure target achievement |
| P&L Fiscal Year | Review annual financial performance |
| P&L Fiscal Months | Understand monthly trends |
| P&L by Market | Compare regional profitability |
| GM% by Quarters | Monitor gross margin trends |

### Key Findings

- Net sales increased from ₹87.5M in 2019 to ₹598.9M in 2021.
- Gross Margin reached ₹218.2M in FY 2021.
- Overall business growth exceeded 300% over three years.
- Japan and New Zealand reported the strongest profit margins.
- Market performance finished 9.2% below annual targets, highlighting improvement opportunities.

---

## Overall Insights

- Strong customer growth accelerated revenue performance.
- New product launches played a major role in business expansion.
- India, the United States, and South Korea emerged as the leading markets.
- Division-level reporting highlighted exceptional growth within Personal Computers.
- Regional profitability analysis supported future strategic planning.
